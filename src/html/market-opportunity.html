Data Sources & Assumptions:
Toyota annual production: ~10.5M units (2024, including Daihatsu and Hino subsidiaries — Toyota Motor Corporation official reports / Statista).
BYD annual production: 4.304M units (2024 — BYD official sales report, January 2025). BYD growth projection to 5.5M by 2026 based on reported capacity of 5.82M and stated 2025 sales targets of 5–6M units.
Ford US volume: 2.2M wholesale units (2024 — Ford Motor Company Form 10-K, SEC filing, January 2025). Ford US is Ford's single largest market representing ~49% of global volume.
Global new vehicle production: ~80M units/year (OICA 2024 estimate).
Minting fee: $7 per vehicle used in chart calculations as an illustrative midpoint. Actual volume tiers: Ultra-scale OEM (3M+/yr) $3–5, Large OEM (1M–3M/yr) $8–12, Mid OEM (100K–1M/yr) $12–18, Small/early adopter (<100K/yr) $20–25. All fees billed in fiat currency (USD/GBP/EUR) — no crypto wallet required from manufacturers or any other payer.
API verification revenue (not shown in these charts): See full 5-stream financial model in whitepaper — insurer/lender API, dealer portal subscriptions, consumer platform, and enterprise & government contracts add materially to the minting revenue shown here.
All financial figures are speculative projections based on indicative assumptions. Actual revenue varies materially by region, regulatory environment, OEM adoption rates, and commercial negotiations. Not a financial forecast.
